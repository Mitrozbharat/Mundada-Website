Leadership

Mundada Polymers is guided by experienced leadership with a vision for excellence. The company is led by Mr. Pramod Mundada, whose entrepreneurial journey began in 2000 with the establishment of Bai-Kakaji Plastics. Under his guidance, the business has grown from a small-scale operation to a diversified group with a focus on innovation, sustainability, and customer service.

With over two decades of industry experience, Mr. Mundada brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to share with our repeat customers. Our leaders are driven by a commitment to quality, transparency, and the continuous improvement of both our products and the decision-making that makes customers trust our packaging solutions and partnerships.
