Manufacturing Setup

A gear pump is a type of positive displacement pump commonly used in various manufacturing processes. It consists of two or more interlocking gears that rotate within a housing or casing. As the gears rotate, they create a vacuum at the inlet, allowing fluid to enter the pump. The fluid is then carried between the gear teeth and the pump casing, finally being discharged at the outlet.
