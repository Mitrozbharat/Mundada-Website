About Raviraj Hi Tech

Raviraj Hi tech is one of the leading manufactures, suppliers and exporters of ferrous and non ferrous metal parts, PDC & GDC casting components. Apart from this, we also cater to the requirements of our customer for precision turn components, assemblies for switchgear for auto electrical industries. Designed in strict conformation with international quality standards, these are known for features like resistance to corrosion, enhance durability and application specific design.


Our Hi Tech manufacturing unit comprising machines like Mazak CNC turning, HASS make VMC, Citizen (Japan) Bar feed, Swiss Automats and various press machines enables us to make various components within promised time.


Owing to the efforts of our professionals, we have been able to build close business relationships with our customers spread across the globe.


In addition we are having Alkaline and Hard Chrome plating plant in house.


Quality awareness coupled with the timely delivery has helped us cater to the demands of Auto Mobile, Auto Electrical and Switchgear industries.
