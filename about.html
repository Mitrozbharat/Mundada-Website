Our Journey

Founded in 2022 as part of the Bai-Kakaji Group’s expansion, Mundada Polymers builds on a legacy of excellence that dates back to the group’s inception in 2000. As a new venture backed by decades of industry expertise, we have rapidly grown into a trusted provider of protective and specialty polymer films. Headquartered in Latur, Maharashtra, we combine innovation and quality to redefine your packaging experience. Today, with a production capacity of over 400 metric tons, Mundada Polymers offers a diverse range of flexible packaging solutions to meet the needs of various industries. From humble beginnings to a fast-emerging industry player, our journey reflects continuous growth, customer trust, and a commitment to setting new benchmarks in packaging excellence.
